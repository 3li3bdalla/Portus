Small update: 2.1.1 released and planning for 2.2

Posted on October 18, 2016 by Miquel Sabaté Solà

After the release of Portus 2.1 some users notified us about some regressions and bugs. We’ve been handling those as well as including some small improvements. You can read more about that in the release notes. We urge users of the 2.1 release to upgrade to 2.1.1 as soon as possible.

The road ahead

As we wrote in the last blog post, we are not particularly happy on how we handled some things during the development of the 2.1 release. For this reason, we will maintain this dedicated page explaining what to expect from new releases, the exact dates, and the overall goals of the releases to come.
